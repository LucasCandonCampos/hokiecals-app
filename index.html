How It Works

From dining hall to logged in three steps

1

Set Your Goals

Enter your height, weight, age, and activity level. HokieCals calculates your personalized daily calorie and macro targets automatically.

2

Browse the Menu

Open the app, select your dining hall, and browse today's live menu. Every item already has its full nutrition data loaded and ready.

3

Tap to Log

Tap the items you ate and they're instantly added to your daily log. Your calorie and macro totals update in real time.

4

Track Your Progress

Check your daily dashboard, log your weight, and review weekly summaries to stay consistent and reach your health goals.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HokieCals free to download?
Yes, HokieCals is free to download on the App Store. Core features including VT dining hall logging are available at no cost.
Which dining halls are supported?
HokieCals supports all major Virginia Tech dining locations, including West End Market, D2 (Dietrick), Turner Place, Hokie Grill, and more. We update the menu data regularly.
How is my calorie goal calculated?
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor formula — the same industry-standard method used by MyFitnessPal and other leading nutrition apps — combined with your activity level and personal goals.
Can I log food from off-campus restaurants?
Yes. HokieCals includes a barcode scanner for packaged foods and a general food database for logging meals from anywhere, not just VT dining halls.
How do I delete my account or data?
You can request account deletion or data removal by emailing us at hokiecals@gmail.com. We will process your request within 7 days.
I found a menu item that's missing or incorrect. What should I do?
Please email us at hokiecals@gmail.com with the details and we'll get it corrected as quickly as possible.
